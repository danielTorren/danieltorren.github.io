About Me

I am a PhD graduate in Climate Economics from ICTA- Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona and Ca' Foscari University of Venice. My research focuses on using Agent-Based Modelling (ABM) to explore the co-evolutionary dynamics of social, cultural, and technological change in the context of the low-carbon transition. I'm originally from Barcelona but grew up in London. Outside of work I enjoy Bikepacking, thru-hiking, bouldering and film photography.

Research Interests

  • Decarbonisation: Positive Tipping points, behavioural and system change
  • Computational Economics: Agent-Based Modelling (ABM), social learning, and network dynamics.
  • Climate Policy: Carbon pricing, endogenous preferences and consumption shifts.
