About Me

I am a PhD graduate in Climate Economics from ICTA-UAB and Ca' Foscari University of Venice. My research focuses on using Agent-Based Modelling (ABM) to explore the co-evolutionary dynamics of social, cultural, and technological change in the context of the low-carbon transition.

Research Interests

  • Computational Economics: Agent-Based Modelling (ABM), social learning, and network dynamics.
  • Climate Policy: Carbon pricing, behavioral spillovers, and consumption shifts.
  • Complex Systems: Bayesian calibration and sensitivity analysis of non-linear models.
